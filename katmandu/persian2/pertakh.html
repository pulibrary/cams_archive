Modern authors using a takhallus

The takhallus, or pen-name, is identified most often by typographical devices such as parentheses or quotation marks. In determining entry element and additional elements for the heading, the takhallus is ignored.

References should be made with the takhallus as the entry element, and all other elements, including the name(s) chosen as surnames for the heading.

Example:

    t.p. form: Mahdi Shirazi "Naqavi"
    heading: Shirazi, Mahdi
    reference: Naqavi, Mahdi Shirazi
