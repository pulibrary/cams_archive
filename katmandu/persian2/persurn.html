Types of surnames

Persian personal names may have single or multiple surname elements and appear on title pages as follows:

Single surnames: Muhammadi; Ahsani; Muzhgan

Surnames with affixes: Bahramzadah; Aryanpur; Kiyanfar, etc. (see list of affixes)

Compound surnames: Darya Bandari; Shariat Panahi; Mushfiq Kashani

In Persian, affixes are not true surnames and should not be considered as independent name elements although you will find some treated as such in the NAF. The following listing includes some examples of Persian affixes.
