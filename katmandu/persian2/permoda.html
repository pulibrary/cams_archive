Persian NACO Manual

Modern Arabs writing in Persian

A modern Arab author writing predominantly in Persian should be established in Persian, with Persian romanization, but according to Arabic entry.

Rules for Arabic name entry call for entering authors under single surname. References may be made from surnames other than the last one listed.

t.p. form: Muhammad Riza al-Alavi al-Dimashqi
100 20 Dimashqi, Muhammad Riza al-Alavi
400 20 Alavi, Muhammad Riza
