References from Takhallus

A reference should be made from the takhallus for modern Persian authors whose chief source form of name includes one. All other elements, including the entry element chosen for the heading should be included in the reference.

Example:

    t.p. form: Mahdi Shirazi "Naqavi"
    100 10: Shirazi, Mahdi
    400 10: Naqavi, Mahdi Shirazi
