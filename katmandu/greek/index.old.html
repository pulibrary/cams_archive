Welcome!

This web site is designed as an aid for people working with Modern Greek text. It is not designed to teach cataloging; cataloging rules and policy are available elsewhere. This page is for a person who works with Modern Greek books; who inputs bibliographic transcription and needs to distinguish relevant bibliographic information and corresponding fields. As the site grows and develops, it is hoped that the information found here, will also please and instruct the reader about Greek culture and language.
