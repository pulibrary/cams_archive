Greek Cities (ancient and modern) of Asia Minor, Pontus and eastern Thrace with their corresponding Turkish names.

The following table of names was reproduced from Vasilēs Pan. Kekēs' book, Hē dikia mas Kappadokia (Athēna : Ekdoseis Akritas, 2004). The original list contained only the Greek form of modern and ancient cities. The table is a sampling list of Greek-Turkish names which may be encountered in cataloging. Contributions from specialists in Turkish history are invited and input would be most welcome, particularly to resolve the blank boxes where no corresponding Turkish form has been found.
