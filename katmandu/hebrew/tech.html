Technical Information

    To avoid the well-known limitations of HTML with non-roman scripts and LC diacritics, the solution chosen here was to make images of each word and incorporate them into the text.
    The problem is that the images display slightly differently on each browser.
  • To ensure the best results, view the abbreviations in Netscape Navigator 2 or higher, and set your browser for Times New Roman.
  • Size of font varies with each browser and monitor.
