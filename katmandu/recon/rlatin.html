Latin Letters

When searching titles in Latin, be aware of the following letter equivalencies and search using both possibilities:

    Latin form
    (on card)
    Normalized form
    (for online searching)
    v u
    j i
    vv w

When inputting such a record, use the form as it appears on the card and the reviewing cataloger may adjust.
