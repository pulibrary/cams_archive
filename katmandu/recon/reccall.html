Holdings: Call Numbers (CAL or LCAL)

Where to put the call numbers depends on how many there are.
Single Call Number

When there is a single call number, put it in the CALL field.

More than One Call Number

When there is more than one call number, put them in the LCAL fields, corresponding to the correct locations.
In these cases, nothing goes into the CAL field.
