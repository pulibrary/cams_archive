No Copy: Uniform Titles

  • When reconning Uniform titles, transcribe the information as found on the card.
  • Leave the punctuation as on card, but supply subfield codes as appropriate.
  • Eliminate any initial articles.
  • Be careful of Transliterated Titles.
      See: Transliterated Titles
There are various types of Uniform Titles in the Card Catalog, including Main Entries and Collective titles.

Uniform TitleA Uniform Title is normally known by its location directly under the author, and in brackets.

 In other cards, a Uniform Title may be typed or written on a corner of the card.

This information is entered into the 240 Field
.

Uniform Title
Main Entry		A Uniform Title Main Entry is known by the information at the top of the card.

This information is entered into the 130 Field.
