Guidelines for Making Collective Uniform Titles

Works
  • Complete Works
Selections
  • Three or more works in various forms.
  • Three or more works in one form if the author wrote in only one form.
  • Extracts from the works of one person.
Works in a
Single Form
  • Complete works in one particular form, use one of the following forms:
      Correspondence
      Essays
      Novels
      Plays
      Poems
      Prose works
      Short stories
      Speeches
If the item includes three or more, but not all works, add ‡kSelections.
