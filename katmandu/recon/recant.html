Holdings: Notes (ANT or LANT)

Certain notes go into either the ANT or LANT note.
Information that pertains to all locations goes into an ANT note. For items in a single location, all such information goes here.

Information that pertains to specific locations goes into a LANT note.
Care must be taken to determine if the information pertains to a single location, or all locations.
