Recon Post-Cataloging Procedure

  • Produce the record and make a LON printout.
  • Attach the Shelflist card to the LON printout, mark it with your initials and add the date.
  • Record statistics as RECONS on the Statistics Form .
  • Catalog Division Staff:
      Put a LON printout in box for DBIU staff to delete the cardset from the Electronic Card Catalog. The printout will then be forwarded to a reviewing cataloger.
  • DBIU staff:
      Delete the cardset from Electronic Card Catalog, using the shelflist. Put a LON printout in the box for a reviewing cataloger.
