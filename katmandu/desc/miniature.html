 Miniature books

For books that are less than 10 centimeters in height, measure the height in millimeters (2.5D1).

Additionally, add a form subject heading:
  650 _0 |a Miniature books |x Specimens.

Example
  The selected poems of Longfellow.
     300 __ |a 78 p. ;|c 80 mm.
     650 _0 |a Miniature books |x Specimens.
