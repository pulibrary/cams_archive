Cataloging of Princeton theses
The cataloging of Princeton dissertations is abbreviated, but all records must include the following information:
  • All authors must be in the NAF
  • A genre subject heading
  • Special Notes
  • An added entry for the department.
Call numbers are constructed according to local practice. There are also special considerations for supplementary materials and Music theses.
