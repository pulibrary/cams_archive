Citation of Source: Titles

In the 670 for the piece in hand cite only the title(s) immediately preceding the name. In case of Muslim religious authors cite only the highest titles earned by the author during his lifetime. The following titles should always be capitalized:
Shaykh
Imam
Hujjat al-Islam 		Ayat Allah
Sayyid
Ustad
