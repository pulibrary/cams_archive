Persian NACO Manual

Volumes in the 670

670s for booksets must always cite which volume(s) the information is taken from. Once a volume has been cited, any later page numbers refer to that volume unless another volume number is cited.
    v. 1, t.p. (xxx xxx xxx) p. 3 (xxx xxx) v. 3, p. 7 (xxx)
    NOTE: the first two sets of info. are from v. 1.

    v. 1-3, t.p. (xxx xxx xxx)
    NOTE: all info. must appear on the title pages of all three volumes.
