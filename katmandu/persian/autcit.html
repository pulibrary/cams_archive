Citing author's name of the item in hand in the 670, if necessary:

If the work has a nondistinctive title and is entered under a different name from the 1xx field, cite the entry element of that name in full, followed by abbreviated forms for the other elements in that name. Ibn is abbreviated with a lower-case "b". Names with a definite article are abbreviated using the article and the first letter (i.e., "al-Hasan" is abbreviated as "al-H."). All letters retain their diacritics. There are no spaces between abbreviated letters except preceding names with definite articles:
Examples: Baghdadi, M.b. al-H.
Ibn al-Lamish, A.b.Kh.
