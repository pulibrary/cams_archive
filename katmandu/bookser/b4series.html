4xx, 8xx (series)
Adjust series numbering and tracing if necessary, to include the volume being added. Please note that non-consecutively numbered series, may require multiple tracings (see examples).

Example 1:

440 0 Studies of the East Asian Institute ;‡vno. 4
-AV is no. 5 in the series.
Change series statement to:
440 0 Studies of the East Asian Institute ;‡vno. 4-5

Example 2:

440 0 Philosophical papers ;‡vv. 8
-AV is v. 11 in the series
Change series statement and tracing to:
490 1 Philosophical papers ;‡vv. 8, 11
830 0 Philosophical papers ;‡vv. 8.
830 0 Philosophical papers ;‡vv. 11.

Example 3:

490 0 DHEW document ;‡vv. 1-2, 5
-AV is v. 7 in the series.
Change series statement to:
490 0 DHEW document ;‡vv. 1-2, 5, 7
