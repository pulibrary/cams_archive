Item Records
Enum field

Records for booksets contain the volume numbering for the individual piece in the "enum" field of the item record. The numbering should always include the designator indicated by the designator note in the MFHD. Do not put a space between the designator and the number.

Examples:

    Enum: vol.1
    Enum: Bd.3
    Enum: vol.2, pt.1
