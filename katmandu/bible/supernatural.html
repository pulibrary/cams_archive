Supernatural Beings

See also: Personal Names

RI22.19 speaks of "mortals" in the Bible. In the case of supernatural beings, they are treated as entities and are found in the Subject Authority File. There is no ‡c before the qualifier. Also, note the coding for a subject.

Examples:
