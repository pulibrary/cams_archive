Cataloging Biblical Materials

Psalms

A somewhat unique case of Biblical uniform titles involves the Psalms. In order to specify a particular Psalm, use the pattern:
Bible.‡pO.T.‡pPsalms[space][particular Psalm(s) as Roman numeral(s)]

Examples:
Bible.‡pO.T.‡pPsalms CXX
Bible.‡pO.T.‡pPsalms CXX-CXXXIV

N.B.: Even if there is only one Psalm, the uniform title is still in the plural:
Bible.‡pO.T.‡pPsalms...
