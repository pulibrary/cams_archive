Art material for recap location rcppj

Books purchased on fund 1203, Art and Archaeology (the fund number is located in the gutter) as well as gift books from Marquand (SA) going to recap should be sent to rcppj.

Do not use the local Princeton artist numbers.

Do follow regular oversize procedures.

The item type in the item record should be changed to CLOSED.

Duplicates:

No new purchases or gifts are to be added to rcppj if they duplicate a resource anywhere in the library system

Existing duplicates in Princeton's collections may go to rcppj only if the other copy is in rcppa. If there is a copy in SA, ANXA, or any recap location other than rcppa, the duplicate may not be transferred to recap.
