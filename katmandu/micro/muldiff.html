 Multiple Versions: Different Generations of Microforms

When microforms of differing generations (with the exception of master preservation copies) and/or polarities are cataloged together:

  • Use the 533 note to describe the positive service copy.
  • Bring out any special charateristics relating to the particular copies in corresponding LANT ‡a notes using the appropriate phrases described in Bibliographic and Holdings Screens for Multiple Versions.
