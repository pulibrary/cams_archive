Determining Whether a Microform Guide Should be Handled as Accompanying Material or a Separate Publication

In past procedures, the only method used when a guide accompanied a microform was to treat it as a separate publication.

  • When the guide has a separate publisher, and/or is written before or after the publication of the microform to describe the contents of the collection being reproduced, use the procedure for separate publications.
      Microform Guide Issued as a Separate Publication

  • More commonly, when the guide is issued by the microform publisher, and describes the microform version of the collection being reproduced, use the procedure for accompanying material.
      Microform Guides Issued as Accompanying Material
