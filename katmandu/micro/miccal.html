Microforms and Photocopies

Call Numbers for Microforms

Call numbers are chronologically assigned accession numbers.

Before these items are cataloged online, a shelflist card must be prepared, a chronological number must be assigned, and the card must be filed in the proper place in the shelflist to reserve the number.

This card is the permanent shelflist record, and will not be replaced by a printed shelflist card.

  • For instructions on preparing a microform shelflist, see:
      Making Shelflist Cards.
