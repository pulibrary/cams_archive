Western Languages Cataloging Team Overview

The Western Languages Cataloging Team catalogs materials in English, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Slavic, Spanish, Latin, and Greek in all subject areas.

Mission: "We are a multidisciplinary group of professional and paraprofessional catalogers dedicated to supporting the research of the Princeton University community by providing efficient, thorough, and accurate access to the Library's holdings in various formats in Western European languages."
