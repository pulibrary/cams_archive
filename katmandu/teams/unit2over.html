Middle Eastern Languages Team (MELT) Overview

Members of the Middle Eastern Languages Team receive and process ME-language approval plans, and catalog all ME-language material received by the library.

Mission: The Middle Eastern Languages Team (MELT) works together as a diverse community to effectively process Middle Eastern Language materials in all formats and contributes to the goals of Catalog Division to improve services to the University and to patrons worldwide.
