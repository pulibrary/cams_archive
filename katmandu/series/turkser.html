Turkish Series Policy
When Turkish series are encountered, search them in our catalog for a series authority record. If an authority record is found, follow it as usual. If no authority record exists, record the title in a 490 0 and do NOT create an authority record. If the heading has been used on a bib record in our catalog but no authority record exists, refer it to the Authorities Coordinator.
