Re-sort a truncated list

Click any column heading to sort the list by that column
Or
On the View menu, click Sort by > [item]. Various record characteristics are available, depending on the type of list.

For instance, click on the “Date” column heading: an arrow will appear  and the list will re-sort by date, earliest to latest:

Click again on the “Date” column heading: the arrow will reverse  and the records be re-sorted by date, latest to earliest:
