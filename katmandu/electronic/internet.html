Cataloging Internet Resources

Only "officially selected" titles will be cataloged. This includes only titles that have been ordered and paid for, or "free" titles for which cataloging has been requested by the officially-designated selector. We will not catalog titles available on a trial basis for a specified period of time.

**THIS POLICY IS BEING REVIEWED.**
Questions about whether or not to catalog a specific internet resource should be directed to a Team Leader.
