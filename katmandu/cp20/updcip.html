Changes to CIP headings

Change headings on NAF records created from CIP data (670 field, subfield b, begins with "CIP t.p."), if we catalog the same book cited, and the actual t.p. form of the name differs from the CIP heading.

Example
Original record 100 10 Hibbard, Malcolm J.
670 Petrography to petrogenesis, 1994:‡bCIP t.p. (Malcolm J. Hibbard, Dept. of Geo. Sci., Univ. of Nevada, Reno)
Corrected record 100 10 Hibbard, M. J.‡q(Malcolm J.)
400 10 ‡wnne‡aHibbard, Malcolm J.
670 Petrography to petrogenesis, 1994:‡bCIP t.p. (Malcolm J. Hibbard, Dept. of Geo. Sci., Univ. of Nevada, Reno) book t.p. (M.J. Hibbard)
  • Note the use of the ‡wnne coding for the previous AACR2 heading, which is now an acceptable AACR2 reference.
