Special situations: Author or Uniform title main entry

Author main entry (100, 110, 111)

If a serial has a personal, corporate, or conference main entry and the title is generic (e.g. Annual report, Proceedings, etc.), the 670 should take this form:

670 Author. Serial title, date of issue: |b location on piece (data)
e.g.
670 International Council for Science. Year book 2003/2004: |b t.p. (International Council for Science; ICSU)

Uniform title main entry (130)

If a serial publication has both a uniform title and a title proper, substitute the uniform title for the title proper if the uniform title consists of the title proper plus a qualifier.

    670 Uniform title, date of issue: |b location on piece (data)
    e.g.:
    670 Camera notes (New York, N.Y. : 1897), Oct. 1901: |b t.p. (Charles W. Stevens)
