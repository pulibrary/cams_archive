Citing Zirikli

Cite Zirikli using this general format:

670 Zirikli |b ([hdg.]; [full name from body of article]; [dates])

Examples:

670 Zirikli |b (al-Firabri; Muhammad ibn Yusuf ibn Matar, Abu Abd Allah al-Firabri; 231-320/846-932)

670 Zirikli |b (al-Badihi; Ali ibn Muhammad, Abu al-Hasan al-Badihi; d. ca. 380/990)
