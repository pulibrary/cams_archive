How to cite dates from reference sources

The general format for citing dates is to give the miladi dates separated by a hyphen, then a slash and the Gregorian dates separated by a hyphen.

    418-489/1027-1105
    231-320/846-932

If only the death date is available:

    d. 895/1490

Other examples:

    ...-nahwa 895 H=...-nahwa 1390 M is cited as: d. ca. 895/1490

    ...-bad 909 H=...-bad 1503 M is cited as: d. after 909/1503

    al-qarn al-ashar al-Hijri, al-qarn al-sadis ashar al-Miladi is cited as: 10th/16th cent.

    geb. 13. Saban 1277/ 25.2.1861, starb 23. Saban 1315/18.1.1898 is cited as:
      b. 13 Saban 1277/2-25-1861, d. 23 Saban 1315/1-18-1898

    erste Halfte des 19 Jahrh.s is cited as: first half of the 19th cent.
