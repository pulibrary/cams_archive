Citing Kahhalah

Cite Kahhalah using this general format:

670 Kahhalah |b ([Heading]; [full name from body of article]; [dates])

Examples:

670 Kahhalah |b (al-Husayn al-Tabari; al-Husayn ibn Ali ibn al-Husayn al-Tabari, al-Shafii, Abu Abd Allah; 418-489/1027-1105)

670 Kahhalah |b (al-Husayn al-Maghribi; al-Husayn ibn Ali ibn al-Husayn ibn Ali ibn Muhammad ibn Yusuf ibn Bahr ibn Bahram al-maruf bi-al-Wazir al-Maghribi wa-bi-Ibn al-Maghribi, Abu al-Qasim; 370-318/980-1027)
