Eligible Headings
All personal and corporate body name headings, and name headings for geographic jurisdictions (see: geographic name headings), are eligible for NACO. This includes names used in series headings as qualifiers. We will not contribute names used only in references on local series authority records.

If you wish to contribute an authority record for a name-title or uniform title heading (other than series), please consult the special section on name/title headings.

In cases of doubt about the eligibility of a name heading for NACO, see dividing up the world.
